# 💎 LUNIM: Beginner's Guide to the Revenue & Rights Dashboard

Welcome to **LUNIM**, the next-generation platform for creative rights management and automated revenue distribution. This guide will help you navigate the dashboard, connect your wallet, and understand how your earnings are tracked on the blockchain.

---

## 🚀 Quick Start: Connecting Your Wallet

LUNIM supports two primary modes of operation to accommodate both development/testing and real-world transactions.

### 1. Live Mode (Real Wallets)
To use LUNIM with your actual assets, click the **"Connect Wallet"** button in the top navigation bar.
- **Supported Wallets:** MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, Trust Wallet, and any EIP-6963 compatible browser extension.
- **Networks:** Ethereum Mainnet, Polygon, and Base Sepolia (Testnet).
- **Features:** Once connected, your wallet address and balance will automatically sync across the entire platform. Your profile icon will reflect your connected status with a live indicator.

### 2. Demo Mode (Local Testing)
If you are a developer or exploring the platform locally, click the **"✨ Web3 Demo"** button.
- **Purpose:** This connects the dashboard to a local **Hardhat Node** (Chain ID: 31337).
- **Automatic Sync:** It will attempt to switch your provider to the local network and link to the simulated revenue splitter contract.
- **Test Accounts:** Use the pre-funded accounts provided by your local Hardhat node to simulate multi-party distributions without spending real gas.

## 📊 Understanding the Dashboard

The dashboard is divided into several key sections designed for transparency and ease of use.

### 💰 Revenue Snapshot
This card provides a high-level view of your financial performance.
- **Total Revenue:** The total amount generated by all your projects.
- **Ready to Release:** Funds currently held in the Smart Contract that are ready to be withdrawn to your wallet.
- **Last Distribution:** Date and time of the most recent payout.

### 📜 Rights & Projects
Manage your creative portfolio and see exactly what percentage of revenue is allocated to you for each work.
- **Revenue Share:** Your ownership percentage in a specific project.
- **Project Nodes:** Visual representation of how funds flow from the project to the contributors.

### ⚡ Smart Contract Panel (Admin Only)
For project managers and platform admins, this panel allows direct interaction with the blockchain logic.
- **Send ETH to Contract:** Link a real-world payment (in USD) to a blockchain transaction to fund the distribution pool.
- **Release Payments:** Trigger the `releasePayments()` function to distribute all accumulated funds to rights holders according to their shares.

---

## 🛠 Troubleshooting & Best Practices

- **Network Mismatch:** If the dashboard shows a "Switch Required" badge, click it to automatically switch your wallet to the correct network for the current project.
- **Synchronization:** If your balance doesn't update immediately after a transaction, use the **"Refresh"** button in the Smart Contract Panel.
- **Wallet Persistence:** LUNIM will remember your connected wallet address for the duration of your session. If you refresh the page, it will automatically attempt to reconnect via your browser extension.
